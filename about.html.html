About Φ-Mapping

← Back to Home

Φ-Mapping quantifies symbolic entropy (Ssym) and torsion field coherence in DNA nanostructures, logic gates, and synthetic circuits—providing a thermodynamic risk score before synthesis.

Key Features

  • ✅ Predicts structural fragility & synthesis failure
  • ✅ Scores topological designs (linear vs. branched vs. cyclic)
  • ✅ Outputs compliance score (0–100 Φ-units)
  • ✅ Integrates via API or standalone CLI

What It Is NOT

  • Not a simulator
  • Not a sequence optimizer
  • Not a theoretical framework
